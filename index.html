Who are we?

Large family run state-of-the-art fitness centre based in Newport, South Wales. We have professional, friendly and experienced staff to help you reach your goals, regardless of current or past abilities.
Visit us to discuss your goals to begin living a healthier, better life!

Extensive range of equipment.

  • Array of Olympic Barbells, Hammer Strength machines, cable systems and dumbbell rack up to 65kg.
  • Numerous power racks, half racks, smith machines and lifting platforms.
  • Dedicated CrossFit area for WODs, mobility sessions and Olympic Weightlifting.
  • Multiple studios for classes such as Spin, Yoga, Pilates and more.

Other facilities onsite

  • Fully qualified physiotherapist for musculoskeletal injury assessment and treatment, mobility and posture analysis with corrective plans.
  • Cafe selling delicious high protein bites, tailored for bulking, cutting, health or just guilt free snacking.
  • Shop stocking all the latest pre-workouts, protein powders, amino acids and other supplements.
  • Privacy-conscious changing facilities and free parking all day.


COVID-19 Precautions

For everyone's protection, please follow the rules set out below:

Please wear a face covering when entering and exiting the gym, browsing the shop and when using communal areas such as the changing rooms.

Wash your hands regularly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or by using the alcohol-based hand sanitizers located throughout.

If you have a persistent cough, please follow the governments advise regarding testing and isolation procedures.
